HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

The Idea of the establishment of Enlightment-Schools came into existence during the early 2009.This idea came about after an incidence where a certain kid committed suicide just because of failing his Ordinary-level Final Exams.This idea was then presented to the legitmate sponsors and they approved it. Finally,In 2012,20th January Enlightment-Schools was established.

LEVELS OF EDUCATION OFFERED

In the Main-campus of Enlightment-Schools situated at Salasala,the School only offers Ordinary-level education and therefore amits only Odinary-level students.Since the emergence of outstanding Alumni from the school,there has been an increament in the number of sponsors.Therefore,the school is planning on developing an Advanced-level Campus and ultimately spreading all over the country and the continent at Large.

VISION OF THE SCHOOL

It is undeniable fact that most schools are conservative and they continue with the traditional methods of judging students' abilities on a piece of paper which is similar to a doctor prescribing the same medicine to all of his or her patients.With over 20+ Staff Members and over 6+ subjects taught,Our school aims to unlock the true potential of children via boosting their creativity and imagination and emphasizing collaboration among them instead of competition.Welcome to Enlightment-Schools, A place where a fish is no longer forced to climb a tree.
